Health Tips 💡

Is irregular period normal?

Sometimes yes. Stress, diet, or hormones can affect your cycle. But if it keeps happening, consider seeing a doctor.

How to manage cramps?

Use warm compress, drink water, rest well, and consider mild pain relief if needed.

When should I see a doctor?

If your period is very painful, extremely heavy, or absent for months, it's best to get checked.

Why do moods change?

Hormones affect emotions. It’s normal to feel different. Rest and self-care help a lot.

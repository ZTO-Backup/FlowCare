Cycle Guide

Understand your body rhythm 🌸

Your menstrual cycle starts on the first day of your period (Day 1). Understanding your cycle helps you stay prepared and make informed decisions.

🩸 Menstrual Phase (Day 1–5)

Bleeding occurs. Energy may be low. Rest is important.

🌱 Follicular Phase (Day 6–13)

Energy increases. Mood improves. Body prepares for ovulation.

🌼 Ovulation Phase (~Day 14)

Ovulation occurs. This is the time of higher fertility. You may feel more active or sensual due to hormonal changes.

🌙 Luteal Phase (Day 15–28)

Body prepares for next cycle. You may experience PMS symptoms like mood changes or fatigue.

⚠️ Fertility Awareness

The days leading up to ovulation and the ovulation day have a higher chance of pregnancy.

If you are not planning pregnancy, consider protection during this time.

🟢 Lower Chance Days

Days after ovulation generally have a lower chance of pregnancy, but not zero risk.

❤️ Body & Emotions

Hormones can affect mood, energy, and desire. Feeling more emotional or sensual at certain times is normal.

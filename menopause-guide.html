📘 What is Menopause?

Menopause is a natural phase where menstrual cycles stop permanently. Understanding it helps you stay prepared and in control.

🧠 Stages of Menopause

Perimenopause: Hormonal changes begin.

Menopause: No period for 12 months.

Post-Menopause: Body adapts.

⚠️ Pre-Menopause Signs

  • Irregular periods
  • Hot flashes
  • Mood swings
  • Sleep problems
  • Fatigue
  • Libido changes

🌸 Post-Menopause Signs

  • Dry skin
  • Bone weakness
  • Weight gain
  • Low estrogen
  • Vaginal dryness

🧍 Physical Changes

  • Hot flashes
  • Joint pain
  • Hair thinning
  • Weight changes

💭 Emotional Changes

  • Mood swings
  • Anxiety
  • Irritability
  • Low motivation

❤️ Sexual Health

Libido may increase or decrease. Vaginal dryness may occur. This is normal and manageable.

💡 Care & Lifestyle

  • Balanced diet (calcium & vitamins)
  • Exercise regularly
  • Good sleep
  • Stress management

🏠 DIY Relief Tips

  • Warm baths
  • Breathing exercises
  • Herbal teas
  • Light exercise

⚠️ When to See a Doctor

  • Severe pain
  • Heavy bleeding after menopause
  • Extreme mood changes
  • Persistent discomfort

❓ FAQs

Is menopause natural? Yes.

Can I get pregnant? Only before menopause.

Does it affect sex? Yes, but manageable.

Is weight gain normal? Yes.

How long does it last? Varies.

Should I keep tracking? Yes.
